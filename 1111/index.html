Power your profits with SUNNIFY

Sunnify merges the growing renewable energy sector with blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies, providing both environmental impact and strong investment potential. Through large-scale solar photovoltaic (PV) installations, Sunnify generates clean energy and channels the resulting revenue back into its ecosystem via the SUNNIFY token.

The SUNNIFY token is a utility token that allows you to invest in solar energy projects and earn rewards based on the electricity generated. By owning SUNNIFY tokens, you're not just investing in a greener future, you're also unlocking the potential for profit through a unique crypto-based reward system.

Token holders can retire SUNNIFY tokens and get a unique NFT certification stating how much CO2 they saved by doing so. The NFT is transferred to their wallet and can be traded on all leading NFT marketplaces.

Our
proven expertise

Over the years, we have built several warehouses of more than 1000 square meters ourselves and rented them out on a long-term basis. More than 400 photovoltaic systems have been built and put into operation, from small to large scale systems. We have already secured 3 areas available to produce around 12 million kWh of electricity annually, generating steady revenue streams from energy sales​, giving us a reliable starting point. We are ready to quickly expand our project portfolio within a short timeframe,  allowing us to initiate projects with a total of 20 MW capacity in just one year.

Sunnify is supported by German banks including Volksbank, Sparkasse and Deutsche Bank that provide additional liquidity with the successful completion of each consecutive project. This allows us to continuously grow our project portfolio and ecosystem and channel more funds into the SUNNIFY token, resulting in increasing investor rewards.
